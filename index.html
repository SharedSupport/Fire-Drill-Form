Generated Summary Result

An awake fire drill was conducted on Monday with a simulated fire located in the staff room. The staff room smoke detector was activated. Daniel was in his living room when the alarm activated. The living room smoke detector also activated. Daniel went out of the living room to the front door to exit the home. Daniel was responsive to the alarms but needed verbal prompting from his staff to help him safely and quickly evacuate to his meeting spot. He exited independently after the verbal cue. Daniel met at the designated meeting spot at the mailbox at end of driveway. Total evacuation time was 0 min 42 sec.

What NOT To Write
Evacuation RouteWent out the front door.
ResponseDid good.

Vague answers like these produce a poor narrative summary. Licensors expect to see where each person was, which route they took, which exit was used, and what level of assistance was needed.
