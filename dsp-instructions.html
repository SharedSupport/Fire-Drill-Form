Choosing the Right Form Type

Open the Fire Drills app on your iPad (tap the icon shown above). When the page loads, tap the form type that applies:

Initial Drill

Use for the first drill after a scheduled period. Includes full safety equipment checks (smoke alarms, extinguishers, water temperature, emergency numbers).

Follow-Up Drill

Use when a previous drill had issues that required follow-up. Skips most safety checks and only asks whether all equipment is now operative.

Completing the Initial Drill Form

Section 1: Site and Timing

FieldWhat to Enter
Site AddressFull address of the home where the drill was conducted
Drill TypeTap Awake or Asleep to indicate whether individuals were awake or asleep when the drill began
Day of WeekSelect the day from the dropdown
DateTap the date field and select today's date (or the date the drill occurred)
Time StartedEnter the time the drill began (hour and minute)
Evacuation TimeEnter how many minutes and seconds it took everyone to exit

Section 2: Drill Details

FieldWhat to Enter
Location of Simulated FireWhere the fire was pretended to be (e.g., "kitchen stove")
Alarm Set Off AtWhere the alarm was activated (e.g., "hallway pull station")
Evacuation RouteDescribe the path used to exit (e.g., "front door to driveway")
Meeting SpotWhere everyone gathered outside (e.g., "end of driveway")

Section 3: Individual Response

FieldWhat to Enter
Individual ResponseDescribe how the individual(s) responded during the drill (e.g., "walked calmly to exit with verbal prompts")
Met at Designated Spot?Tap Yes or No. If No, explain why in the box that appears.

Section 4: Participants

  1. Under Individuals, type each person's full name and tap Add. Add all individuals who participated.
  2. Under Staff Present, add each staff member's name the same way.
  3. In Reporting Staff, type your own full name.

Section 5: Safety Equipment Checks

FieldWhat to Enter
Areas of ConcernTap Yes if anything needs attention, then describe. Tap No if all was fine.
Smoke Alarms OKTap Yes or No. If No, describe the issue.
Extinguishers OKTap Yes or No. If No, describe the issue.
Water Temp Below 120 degrees F?Tap Yes or No. If No, enter a follow-up date.
Actual Water TemperatureEnter the temperature reading from the tap (e.g., 112 degrees F). Always fill this in.
Emergency Numbers Posted?Tap Yes or No. If No, enter the date it was corrected.

Section 6: Manager Evaluation (if applicable)

If a manager was present, they may complete the evacuation time evaluation, emergency plan evaluation, and overall rating. If not applicable, leave blank.

Completing the Follow-Up Drill Form

The Follow-Up form is shorter. Fill in all the same Site and Timing, Drill Details, Individual Response, and Participants sections as above. The only difference is the equipment section:

  • Tap Yes or No for All Equipment Now Operative.
  • If No, describe the remaining issues in the box that appears.

Submitting the Form

  1. Review your entries and scroll through to make sure nothing is blank.
  2. Tap the Submit and Send Report button at the bottom.
  3. A confirmation message will appear. The report is automatically emailed as a PDF to the compliance team.
Do not tap Submit more than once. Each tap sends a separate email. Wait for the confirmation message before closing the app.
No printer needed. The PDF is generated automatically and sent to the compliance team. You do not need to print or save anything.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What if I made a mistake after submitting? Contact Michele Treas or Chrissy Strauser and let them know. They will receive the report and can note the correction.
  • What if the app will not load? Check that the iPad has internet access. If the problem continues, contact your supervisor.
  • Do I need to fill out a paper form too? No. This form replaces the paper process. The PDF is kept on file automatically.
  • What if an individual was not home during the drill? Only list individuals who were present. Note any absences in the narrative/response field if needed.
